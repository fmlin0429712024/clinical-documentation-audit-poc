# Safety and Governance Notes

## Public-Safety Design

This POC was created independently using fictional mock data. It does not reproduce any client record, workflow documentation, source schema, audit policy, implementation artifact, proprietary terminology, or production metric.

## Intended Role of AI

AI may organize evidence and draft a traceable question for review. A qualified human retains ownership of clinical interpretation, coding, compliance, and any downstream action.

## Production Readiness Questions

Before any real deployment, a team would need to establish data access controls, privacy review, clinical governance, versioned policy management, evaluation criteria, audit logging, escalation paths, and clear operating ownership.
